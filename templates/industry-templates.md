# Industry Templates

## SaaS
**Tables:** subscriptions, customers, usage_events, invoices

**Key Metrics:**
- MRR/ARR trends
- Churn rate
- Feature usage
- Customer lifetime value

**Sample Queries:**
- "What's our MRR trend?"
- "Which customers are at churn risk?"
- "Top features by usage?"

---

## Retail
**Tables:** orders, customers, products, inventory

**Key Metrics:**
- Revenue by category
- Average order value
- Inventory turnover
- Customer segments

**Sample Queries:**
- "What's our revenue by category?"
- "Who are our top customers?"
- "Which products need restocking?"
